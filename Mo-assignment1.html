Tested on Brain and Behavior

Scientist at Brain.fm work with collaborators at academic
institutions to run experiments.

We look at effects of our technology on the brain, using
fMRI and EEG, and also run large-scale behavioral tests,
measuring performance on simple games.

Our experiments always include a controlled condition
('placebo') of the same underlying music without Brain.fm
technology applied, so we can be sure our tech is what makes
the difference.
